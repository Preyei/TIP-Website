About Us


Mission


The Intentional Parent Academy exists to transform the global parenting narrative by equipping parents to become intentional, value‑driven impact makers. Through carefully curated courses and coaching programs, we empower parents to raise children with clarity, confidence, and purpose.


Vision


The Intentional Parent Academy envisions a world where conscious, value‑based parenting shapes homes, strengthens families, and positively influences future generations. We aim to cultivate a global community of parents who lead with intention and create lasting impact starting from themselves.
