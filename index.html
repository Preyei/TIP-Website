About Us


Mission


The Intentional Parent Academy exists to transform the global parenting narrative by equipping parents to become intentional, value‑driven impact makers. Through carefully curated courses and coaching programs, we empower parents to raise children with clarity, confidence, and purpose.


Vision


The Intentional Parent Academy envisions a world where conscious, value‑based parenting shapes homes, strengthens families, and positively influences future generations. We aim to cultivate a global community of parents who lead with intention and create lasting impact starting from themselves.


Values


  • Intentionality
  • Global Impact
  • Value-driven Parenting
  • Impact Leadership
  • Continuous Growth
  • Awareness
  • Child Centred Development


Profiles

Coaches

Coach Wendy Ologe

Wendy Ologe is the foremost parent coach in Africa who believes that her calling to parents will change the next generation of people in Africa. She is the founder of The Intentional Parent Academy; a coaching outfit dedicated to equipping parents and intending parents to intentionally raise their children positively especially in the face of our decaying societal values. She aims to equip parents with everything it takes to raise an excellent child mentally, physically, spiritually, emotionally, and psychologically.

Coach Kunle Ologe

Kunle Ologe has a passion for developing people, especially the youth, as he has discovered that if directed properly early enough, people will more often than not utilize their God-given talents and contribute meaningfully to the society. He is a Marriage Counselor and author of the book, "Building Your Marriage Plan." He is the lead coach at The Marriage Seal Academy. He founded the family hub with his wife, Wendy.

Coach Ebuka Ede

Ebuka Ede is a Boys Development Coach. He mentors, coaches, and trains boys in life, leadership, and purpose with his wife, Coach Ruth. He oversees the holistic development of boys and advocates for their well-being. As the founder of Save the Boys Initiative and convener of Boys Leadership Academy, they have taught boys, youth, and men for over a decade in a unique, practical, and humorous way to learn personal responsibility and leadership.

Coach Etimah Umeh

Etima Umeh is a Teens Mentor with nearly 2 decades’ experience. Her purpose is to raise responsible teenagers balanced in all areas of life. This passion drives her to partner with parents to coach, mentor, train, and build capacity among youths.
